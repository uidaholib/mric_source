Malcolm M. Renfrew Interdisciplinary Colloquium is a long standing lecture series by distinguished members of the University of Idaho community presenting and describing their approach to teaching and research in their disciplines. This web archive preserves access to a record of past lectures as presented on the MRIC website, often including an abstract and video recordings. For information about MRIC and this project, please visit the About page.

The archive contains more than 240 items that can searched in the Database or by Google Custom Search below.
